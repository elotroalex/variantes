Bienvenidos! Bem-vindos!

Welcome to our inaugural issue of Variantes, the graduate student-run literary and fine arts magazine of the Department of Spanish and Portuguese at Yale Universtiy. Our magazine showcases textual, visual and digital arts related to the languages and culture of the Iberian Peninsula and the Americas. We invite you to submit your work for consideration for future issues, and hope you enjoy our selections.
